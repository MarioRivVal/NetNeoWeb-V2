Student Management

This project was developed for educator who entrusted me with the task. Following the Model-View-Controller (MVC) architecture and leveraging the power of classes, I designed this application. It seamlessly utilizes IndexedDB, making it a fully-fledged Progressive Web App (PWA).

The primary objective is to facilitate the management of student records, including detailed information and payment tracking. Periodically, every three months, it automatically identifies and generates bills for students who have made payments, streamlining the administrative process for the teacher.
